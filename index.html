Artificial Intelligence and Cybernetics (AGenCy) lab at the Independent University Bangladesh (IUB), was established in 2010 (previously known as Computer Vision and Cybernetics Research Lab), is dedicated to education and research in the theory and application of Artificial Intelligence and Cybernetics. This includes development of computational models for data representation, visualization & analysis using probabilistic machine learning, deep learning, and statistical Inference. These models are applied successfully in IoT & Robotics, HCI & Social Computing, Education, Agriculture, Cognitive Neuroscience, Visual & Auditory data analysis, Rehabilitation and Sports Technology, Bioinformatics, Biometrics, Bangla NLP, Particle & Astro-physical data analytics, and Environmental and Geospatial data analytics. AGenCy Lab@IUB is a group composed of faculty, graduate and undergraduate students from the computer science and engineering, electrical engineering, physics, mathematics, economics, environmental science departments from IUB and other renowned universities from home and abroad.
